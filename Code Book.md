Column Descriptions for final results:

subjectID
	Ranges between 1 and 30 to represent all subjects that were tested in the training and testing phase

activityname
	There are 6 activities performed by each subject
		WALKING
		WALKING_UPSTAIRS
		WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
		SITTING
		STANDING
		LAYING
